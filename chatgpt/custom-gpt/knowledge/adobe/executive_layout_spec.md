# Executive Layout Spec

Apply this layout policy to produce C-suite-ready legal PDFs.

## Page and Grid
1. Use A4 portrait by default.
2. Maintain comfortable margins suitable for print and digital review.
3. Keep section transitions explicit with title bars or divider lines.

## Typographic Hierarchy
1. Use a formal title style for section headers.
2. Use body typography optimized for dense legal reading.
3. Keep line spacing and paragraph spacing consistent.
4. Keep bullet lists short and decision-oriented.

## Required Sections
1. Document title and confidentiality label.
2. Executive summary (outcome-oriented).
3. Strategic priorities.
4. Risk matrix with owner accountability.
5. Citation register.
6. Optional annexes.

## Visual Tone
1. Use restrained legal-professional styling.
2. Prioritize readability over decorative effects.
3. Keep contrast high for print and low-quality projectors.

## Compliance Cues
1. Include privilege/confidential markers in visible content.
2. Preserve citation IDs verbatim.
3. Avoid unresolved placeholders in final output.
